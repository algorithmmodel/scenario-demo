Business impact — With ORA vs Without ORA
Without ORA — how the date is set today
Management adds up the "Most Likely" durations of the plan and commits to that date. No critical chain, no probability, no risk concentration is computed by anyone before the commitment.
With ORA — the same plan, analyzed first
The same model, the same data. The ORA computes the probability before the commitment, identifies where the risk concentrates, and recommends a date with a known confidence level.
Without an ORA, these calculations are not performed by anyone in the organization before decisions reach the CEO's desk.
